Clinical Research at Atrium Health Wake Forest

Our Infectious Disease Clinical Research Unit leads innovative, equity-driven studies that advance HIV and STI care. We work with diverse populations to test new treatments, interventions, and delivery models that improve health outcomes and quality of life.

Active Studies

  • HIV Prevention Trials: Evaluating PrEP, long-acting injectables, and microbicides in at-risk populations.
  • STI Screening Innovations: Testing self-collected samples and mobile outreach for underserved communities.
  • Aging with HIV: Investigating comorbidities, cognitive health, and care models for older adults.
  • Community-Based Research: Partnering with local organizations to develop culturally grounded solutions.

How to Participate

We welcome individuals from all backgrounds. Our studies may include surveys, blood tests, interviews, or medications. Participation is voluntary, and many studies provide compensation.
